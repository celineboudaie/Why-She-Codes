About Us


Who We Are:


We're Mary and Celine, KwK Los Angeles 2018 Scholars who created this website!

By using the languages we've learned in two weeks with KodeWithKlossy: Ruby, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Sinatra through Learn and the Flatiron School, we've compiled this website.


Our Mission:


Guided by a shared interest in coding, fashion, beauty, and animals, to tie them all together, we wanted to create an exhaustive source where noncruelty fashion and beauty brands can be found.

We hope everyone can use this website to make the world a kinder place
